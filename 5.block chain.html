BLOCK-CHAIN TECHNOLOGY

Blockchain technology is a structure that stores transactional records, also known as the block, of the public in several databases, known as the “chain,” in a network connected through peer-to-peer nodes. Blockchain technology achieves decentralized security and trust in several ways. The original Blockchain is open-source technology which offers an alternative to the traditional intermediary for transfers of the crypto-currency Bitcoin.

Learn more about Block-Chain Technology

A blockchain network can track orders, payments, accounts, production and much more. Blockchain is a type of shared database that differs from a typical database in the way that it stores information.Technologically, Blockchain is a digital ledger that is gaining a lot of attention and traction recently.
